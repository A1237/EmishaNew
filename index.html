21 - 23 UtkalHacks, 2019 | Bhubaneswar, India

Why UtkalHacks?

There are a lot of Hackathons being hosted every now and then. A lot of Developers and Designers get together and build cool stuffs. But the problem here is, it still sound scary and not so welcoming for the beginners yet! Even those who make it to the finals with some great prototypes, they also fail because their prototypes remains prototype. And the story ends there. Read the Blog post about how we are solving this problem. We are trying to solve this problem by putting up a lot of beginners friendly workshops to on board newcomers. Prior to the Hackathon, participants will be provided with 2 days of training on some of the most important topics like Front end Development, UI / UX etc. by some of the most prominent speakers. During the Hackathon there will be mentorship.
